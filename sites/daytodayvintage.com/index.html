Our Story & Ethos

Our Story

Day to Day Vintage was born from a passion for timeless garments and the vibrant history woven into every stitch. Established in the heart of North Park, San Diego, we meticulously hand-select quality pieces from the 1940s through the 2000s, ensuring every item tells a unique story. (Loading content...)

The Curators

Our founders are lifelong collectors with a keen eye for authenticity and condition. We believe in providing knowledgeable guidance, treating the process of finding your next vintage piece as a shared, personal journey. (Loading content...)

Sourcing & Sustainability

Sustainability is at our core. We ethically source our entire collection, championing slow fashion by giving clothes a second life and reducing environmental impact. Every purchase supports a circular economy. (Loading content...)
