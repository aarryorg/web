The Aarry Scientific CRO Methodology

01

Data Diagnostics and Audit

We start by analyzing existing data to find where the revenue is leaking and what users are struggling with.

  • **GA4 & Analytics Deep Dive:** Identifying funnel drop-offs and high-bounce pages.
  • **User Behavior Mapping:** Using heatmaps and session recordings to see *how* users interact.
  • **Technical UX Score:** Assessing mobile usability, speed, and Core Web Vitals performance.
02

Hypothesis Generation and A/B Design

We translate friction points into statistically sound hypotheses, designing high-impact test variations.

  • **Prioritization:** Ranking test ideas by potential impact and ease of implementation.
  • **Creative Development:** Designing alternate layouts, CTAs, and trust elements.
  • **Statistical Setup:** Ensuring tests run long enough to achieve **95% statistical significance.**
03

Rigorous A/B Testing and Deployment

We deploy and manage the tests, closely monitoring data until a statistically proven winner emerges.

  • **Testing Tools Integration:** Deploying tests through Google Optimize or equivalent platforms.
  • **Traffic Segmentation:** Running tests on specific segments (e.g., Mobile Users, New Visitors) for deeper insight.
  • **Winner Implementation:** Applying the proven winning variant to the live code (or Headless Front End).
04

Continuous Reporting and Scaling

CRO is perpetual. We report on revenue uplift and roll out new tests to ensure exponential growth.

  • **Revenue-Focused Reports:** Clear monthly summaries detailing total $\text{ROI}$ and conversion rate percentage gains.
  • **Documentation:** Creating a knowledge base of test results and learned user behavior.
  • **Perpetual Testing Calendar:** Initiating the next round of high-impact tests immediately.

Conversion Rate Optimization Questions Answered

Understanding the science behind maximizing your existing traffic and revenue.

Methodology and Testing

The initial conversion rate audit and hypothesis testing phase typically takes **4-6 weeks**. Measurable results (revenue uplift) are usually seen within the first **3 months** as winning tests are implemented and scaled across the site.

We focus on high-impact areas: Product Page (A/B testing copy, imagery, and layout), Checkout Flow (reducing friction, optimizing form fields), Homepage/Landing Pages (improving hero and CTA clarity), and Mobile UX (ensuring speed and tap target optimization).

We are platform-agnostic but routinely deploy and manage tests using tools like Google Optimize, VWO, or professional solutions built on Headless architectures. For research, we use Hotjar (heatmaps/recordings), Google Analytics 4, and qualitative customer surveys.

Strategy and SEO Alignment

By improving the user experience (UX) and site speed, CRO directly benefits SEO. Google uses UX signals like reduced bounce rate and increased time-on-page (Dwell Time) as ranking factors. A faster, more usable site that converts better is heavily favored by search engines. This aligns perfectly with our Shopify SEO services.

For sustained, high-level growth, CRO is an **ongoing retainer service**. Conversion rates degrade over time as customer behavior changes. We implement a perpetual testing calendar to ensure you continuously find new conversion wins and maintain market efficiency.

We recommend building sufficient traffic (ideally 15,000+ monthly visitors) before dedicating budget to full CRO. Without enough data volume, A/B tests can't reach statistical significance. For new stores, we focus on foundational UX and technical SEO first.
