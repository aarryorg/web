The Aarry 4-Step SEO-Safe Migration Process

01

Pre-Migration Audit and Mapping

We begin by comprehensively documenting your current store and developing a foolproof migration plan.

  • **Full URL Export:** Collecting every critical URL for **301 redirect mapping.**
  • **Data Integrity Check:** Auditing products, customers, and order history schema.
  • **New Architecture Design:** Defining new Shopify collection and navigation structure.
02

Staging Build, Data Transfer, and QA

Data is securely moved and the new Shopify Plus storefront is built and tested in a private staging environment.

  • **Initial Data Push:** Transferring primary assets (products, categories, content).
  • **Theme Implementation:** Installing and configuring the new (or Headless) Shopify theme.
  • **Transaction QA:** Testing payment gateways, shipping, and fulfillment logic.
03

Final SEO Check and 301 Deployment

This is the most critical stage where we lock in your authority and prevent 404 errors.

  • **Redirect Deployment:** Uploading the complete, verified 1:1 301 redirect map.
  • **On-Page Optimization:** Ensuring all meta titles and descriptions are optimized. (Leveraging our Shopify SEO expertise).
  • **Final Data Sync:** Moving the last batch of customer/order data just before launch.
04

Go-Live, Monitoring and Handoff

The moment of truth: A flawless launch followed by intensive monitoring to catch any post-launch issues.

  • **Domain Switch:** Executing the domain switch in minutes to ensure near-zero downtime.
  • **GSC Monitoring:** Immediate monitoring for crawl errors and indexing issues.
  • **Training & Support:** Full training for your team and 60 days of post-migration support.

Migration Risk Mitigation Questions Answered

Addressing the critical fears around data, traffic, and platform complexity.

SEO and Data Integrity

While Google's algorithm means we cannot promise zero *fluctuation*, **we guarantee zero permanent loss of your hard-earned traffic and authority**. Our exhaustive 301 redirect map (one for every page) and canonicalization audit ensures link equity is preserved during the move.

We perform a comprehensive crawl of your entire live site, map every old URL to its corresponding new Shopify URL (1:1), and bulk upload the verified 301 redirects directly into the Shopify platform before launch. We then monitor for 404s for 60 days post-launch.

Order history, customer names, and addresses transfer seamlessly. Due to security protocols, customer passwords cannot be transferred. Customers will receive a link to securely reset their password upon first login to the new Shopify store.

Process and Investment

The timeline depends on complexity (data volume, custom integrations). A standard migration (WooCommerce/BigCommerce to Shopify Plus) typically takes **8 to 12 weeks**. A complex migration (Magento/Custom Platform with ERP integration) may take **12 to 20 weeks**.

We conduct a **fixed-cost discovery phase** to audit your current store's data and complexity. This allows us to provide a transparent, **fixed-price quote** for the entire migration project, ensuring you face zero scope creep or surprise hourly bills.

We utilize a private staging environment for rigorous $\text{QA}$ over multiple weeks. Testing includes transactional flow (checkout, payments), form submission validation, mobile responsiveness, integration functionality, and a final link check using tools like Screaming Frog to validate all 301 redirects are working.
