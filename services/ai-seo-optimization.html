Technical Foundation

Monolithic systems often struggle with the speed and granular data access AI requires. We start with a technical audit to identify bottlenecks (code bloat, slow APIs) that prevent high Core Web Vitals scores and clean Schema implementation, both critical for GEO.

Schema Markup (structured data) is essential. It feeds facts and relationships directly to the search engine’s Knowledge Graph, which AI models use as a source of truth. Without clean, validated Schema, your data is much less likely to be cited by the AI.

High speed is non-negotiable. Google's AI favors a smooth user experience. We optimize for Core Web Vitals to ensure your site is fast, stable, and responsive, improving both traditional rankings and AI citation probability.

Content & Strategy

AI is a powerful assistant, not a replacement. Generative content must be reviewed, edited, and infused with human Experience (the first 'E' of E-E-A-T) and unique data. We use AI for scaling drafts and optimization, but human experts provide the authority needed for citation.

We shift from simple keywords to **topic authority modeling**. We use advanced tools to identify user *intent gaps* and target long-tail, complex questions the AI struggles to answer, positioning your content as the definitive, easy-to-summarize source.

Clarity and structure. AI models prefer numbered lists, tables, concise definitions, and clear $\text{H}2$/$\text{H}3$ headings. Content must be easily scannable and chunked so the AI can pull out exact answers for its summary. We rewrite content for machine readability first.
